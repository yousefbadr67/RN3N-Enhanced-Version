What is our goal in the long run?

We're the people who actually make an impact in changing the world for the better! At Resalet Nour Ala Nour we employ a holistic approach in our mission to eradicate poverty, and provide a decent and humane standard of living to everyone in need of support, directly and transparently. Join us in our fight to end poverty and improve the lives of millions!

We take pride in our efforts and we look to people like you to support us and help us sustain our efforts as well as expand our efforts to reach more and more struggling lives everyday. Your support means a lot to not just us, but to the entire community.
