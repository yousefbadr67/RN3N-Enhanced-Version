Water for All

We believe in a world where every single person has clean and safe water. Through water connections to houses, water pumping stations, and drilling pure water wells, we're making this vision a reality across Egypt.

Every year, Resalet Nour Ala Nour distributes thousands of Ramadan food boxes in Cairo, Giza, Fayoum, El Menofeya, Edfu, Qena, as well as distributing food boxes in the most remote areas in Egypt.
