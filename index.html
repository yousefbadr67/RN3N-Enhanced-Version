About Us

Resalet Nour Ala Nour (RNAN) is a non-governmental, non-profit, charitable, developmental organization founded by the late renowned media figure, Ahmed Farrag. It was launched on 17/3/2007, registration no.: 2855 under the oversight of the Egyptian Ministry of Social Solidarity. Its headquarters is in Shabramant, Giza, and its various activities cover all governorates of the Arab Republic of Egypt, with subsidiary offices in Giza, Aswan, North Sinai and the Red Sea (Halayeb and Al-Shalateen).

RNAN aims to end poverty in all its forms, empower marginalized, remote and poorer communities, and give real opportunities to families and individuals who are otherwise unable to enjoy a decent human life. It also aims for sustainable development whose impact does not disappear with time or when the external aid stops.

We look at the projects at hand with a long-term view that allows us to strategically plan our way forward and efficiently utilize available resources. RNAN is primarily inspired by the initiatives and unprecedented renaissance that Egypt is currently witnessing.

RNAN's services and projects include the following sectors:
1- Combating poverty and unemployment.
2- Health and medical services.
3- Education and human development.
4- Community and sustainable development.
5- Rapid response to disasters and crisis management.

RNAN looks forward to being an effective partner in the renaissance and development of Egypt. Join us to make life and build a nation!
